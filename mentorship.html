What I help with

Strategy & planning

A clear-eyed look at your current approach — sources, timetable, optional choice, and the gaps you can't see yourself. We build a plan you can actually sustain.

Best for: early-stage aspirants

Answer-writing reviews

You send answers; I return honest, specific feedback on structure, content, and presentation — the three places marks are quietly won or lost.

Best for: Mains preparation

Interview & personality test

Mock discussions and DAF-based preparation to help you speak with clarity and composure when it counts most.

Best for: post-Mains stage

The steady check-in

A recurring conversation to keep you accountable, troubleshoot slumps, and protect your momentum across a long preparation.

Best for: anyone going the distance
How it works
Step 01

Tell me where you are

Reach out through the connect page with a short note on your stage, your attempt history, and what you're stuck on.

Step 02

We find the fit

A short first conversation to see whether I'm the right person to help — and which kind of support actually serves you.

Step 03

We get to work

Structured sessions, honest feedback, and a plan you own. No dependency, no false comfort — just steady progress.

A note on honesty: I take on only as many aspirants as I can genuinely give time to, so availability is limited and sometimes closed. If we're not the right fit, I'll say so — that's part of the help.
