In short

I graduated from IIT Bombay with a dual degree in Civil & Structural Engineering, finishing first in the civil engineering branch and receiving the Institute Silver Medal for academic excellence. Along the way I spent a summer as a research scholar at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, published two peer-reviewed papers in structural engineering, and worked as a structural engineer at ExxonMobil.

Somewhere in those years, the pull toward public service grew stronger than the pull of a settled engineering career. I prepared for the Civil Services Examination alongside work and other commitments, and qualified in CSE 2023. I now serve with the Indian Railways as an officer trainee.

I write and mentor because the preparation taught me as much about endurance as it did about polity or ethics — and because clear guidance was the thing I most wished for when I started.
